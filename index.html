Coffee!

I first started my coffee journey in 2013 by getting a job at my local Peet's Coffee and Tea. I worked at that location from 2013-2016 and learned everything I possibly could from the baristas around me. First came the coffee knowledge, what coffees from different regions tasted like and specific flavor profiles. Second came all of the espresso bar knowledge, how to time shots for the perfect flavor, how to steam milk to the pefect temperature and foam composition and finally how to properly mix drinks so all of the flavor is despersed evenly. Finally came the latte art! one of my favorite parts about working with coffee. It was definitely one of the hardest things to learn to do but once you get the basics down, it becomes almost like second nature, you make hearts on every latte without even thinking about it.
